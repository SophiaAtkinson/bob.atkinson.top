Robert James Atkinson Jr

OCTOBER 10, 1944 - SEPTEMBER 12, 2013

He was born in Portland, Oregon on October 10, 1944 to Robert Sr. and Dorothy (Krumholz) Atkinson. Bob graduated from Wilson High School in Portland, OR in 1963 and married Karen Springer on December 28th 1968. In 1966 Bob was drafted into the US Army and served time in Vietnam from 1967 to 1968. Bob worked as a marine machinist for various Portland based ship yard companies and ended his career in 2002 when he retired. He was a devoted family man and loved the outdoors, especially fishing and hunting. He was a man of simple pleasures but aimed to please all and was always helping others with any job large or small.

Bob is survived by his wife; Karen Atkinson, children; Kevin and Tim, sisters; Peggy Goodrich and Lynda Trost; June his mother-in-law, and 5 grandchildren.
